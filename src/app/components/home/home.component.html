Work at the Olympic Games

What if instead of watching the Games, you could be part of the team that delivered the world’s largest sporting event to billions of viewers around the globe?

JUMPSTART YOUR CAREER

Work behind-the-scenes at the Olympic Games alongside some of the best in the industry

The Broadcast Training Programme (BTP) provides local university students with hands-on experience and insight into how the Olympic Games are broadcast.

We offer training in a variety of broadcast-related roles and the chance to work in a paid position within our team.

This is not a typical work placement — it’s a great opportunity to get your resume off to an incredible start and gain experience that will last a lifetime. There is no better place than the Olympics to kick off your career in broadcasting and event management.


Ready to join the BTP Team?

Positions available in the programme are limited. Sign up to our mailing list and be the first to find out when applications open for Tokyo 2020!

To join the programme, you must be enrolled in a Japanese university. Preference will be given to students attending one of the partner universities.

THE BTP STUDENT EXPERIENCE

By working with us, you will have the opportunity to complete your education with a truly unique work experience

Professional Experience

You will gain valuable experience and build your resume. You will learn from and work with a diverse team of experts in their field while developing new skills and knowledge to prepare you for your future career.

International Exposure

You will interact with people from around the world and learn more about different cultures. You will also have the chance to practice your English skills on a daily basis and improve your confidence.

Connections

You will engage with passionate, high-achieving, experienced professionals, as well as build relationships with other BTP participants. You will be able to expand your network while also maybe making lifelong friends along the way.

OLYMPIC BROADCASTING SERVICES

We are the host broadcaster of the Olympic and Paralympic Games

Olympic Broadcasting Services (OBS) produces the live television, radio and digital coverage of the Olympic and Paralympic Games. Our coverage is neutral, favouring no particular country or athlete, and includes sports competitions as well as the Opening and Closing Ceremonies.

OBS is the broadcasting division of the International Olympic Committee (IOC) and is responsible for the entire planning and delivery of the broadcast coverage of the Games.
